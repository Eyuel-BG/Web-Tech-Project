Our Story

Who We Are

Mentor Knowledge Solutions PLC is an IT and Management Consulting firm taking an active role in the business of making organizations perform better.

It was founded by a group of committed high-level professionals and industrialists, who have the necessary knowledge and experience to make an unsolicited contribution to the provision of technology-enabled innovative business solutions and services.

Our team combines deep sector knowledge with technical excellence to deliver solutions that are not just functional, but truly transformative.

What Drives Us

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading IT and management consulting firm in the region, recognized for our innovative solutions, quality of service, and commitment to client success.

Our Mission

  • Provide proactive and responsive IT and management consulting services that help small and medium enterprises become stronger and more competitive.
  • Offer custom application development services for both enterprise and consumer apps based on business process management concepts.
  • Provide technology-enabled back-office and front-office business services with sourcing arrangement.
  • Create a platform strategy to attract top talent, and foster innovation and creativity in the workplace to retain them.
  • Provide tailored professional development and executive education to improve the skillsets, knowledge and competencies of business executives and IT personnel.
  • Foster entrepreneurship and business incubation.
  • Deliver real value to shareholders through consistent growth and profitability.
Collaboration

WFP CATS Piloting & Review Workshop

Mentor Knowledge Solutions plays an active role in modernizing public distribution systems. A testament to this is our joint collaboration with the World Food Programme (WFP) and the Ethiopian Disaster Risk Management Commission (EDRMC) on the CATS system.

During our training workshops, technologists, government experts, and operations leads align to test commodity logistics modules, ensuring transparency, visibility, and coordination at scale.
